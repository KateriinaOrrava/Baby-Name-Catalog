Names are IMPORTANT

"A child's name is their first identity, the foundation of their sense of self and how they are perceived by others. It is a gift that will stay with them for a lifetime." - Unknown

Baby names are particularly important for several reasons: Individuality: Choosing a baby name allows parents to give their child a unique identity. A name is something that will stay with a child for the rest of their life, and can help them stand out as an individual. Family and cultural heritage: Baby names can reflect a family's cultural or religious heritage, and can be a way to honor ancestors or carry on family traditions. Emotional connection: The process of choosing a baby name can create an emotional connection between parents and their child. It can be a way to express love and affection, and can help parents feel more connected to their child. Social impact: Baby names can also have social impact. Names can be used to challenge gender norms, celebrate diversity, or make a political statement. Overall, baby names are important because they can shape a child's identity and have a lasting impact on their life.
